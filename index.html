About

Established in 2012 and based in Sydney, Australia, Leigh Gainfort is a Level 2 Motorcycling Australia Master Coach and former Motorcycling New South Wales team coach in 2019, 2022 and 2024, training the NSW State team for the Aussie Junior Nationals. He is a former Auckland and NZ motocross champion, and former Amcross champion in 2023, 2022. Leigh still races many State and National level events. He has travelled to the USA and ClubMX to continue to develop his coaching expertise and share the latest coaching techniques.

Leigh's coaching combines decades of racing experience with proven teaching methods. He understands what it takes to excel in motocross, from mastering the fundamentals to developing the advanced techniques that separate good riders from great ones.
