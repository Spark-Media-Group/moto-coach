About

Based in Sydney, Australia, Leigh Gainfort is a Level 2 Motorcycling Australia Master Coach and Motorcycling New South Wales junior team coach, training the NSW State team for the Aussie Junior Nationals. He is a former Auckland and NZ motocross champion, and current Amcross champion. Leigh still races many State and National level events. He has travelled to the USA and ClubMX to continue his coaching training and learn more ways to coach riders.

Leigh's coaching combines decades of racing experience with proven teaching methods. He understands what it takes to excel in motocross, from mastering the fundamentals to developing the advanced techniques that separate good riders from great ones.
