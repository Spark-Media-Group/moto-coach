Why riders stay with Moto Coach

Sharper lines, faster laps, stronger mindset.

Each program is engineered to keep riders composed under pressure. We focus on fundamentals that hold at race pace: body position, throttle control, track reading, and pro-level preparation.

Race-proven structure

Weekly after school blocks at Penrith, Dargle, and Appin keep riders in rhythm. Sessions are capped at twelve so every drill is deliberate and every rider is seen.

Focused small groups

Fast 50 track sessions at Appin are grouped by bike size and capability. Two-hour intensives, a maximum of six riders, and access to public tracks for real-world reps.

Comprehensive support

From Motorcycling NSW Kickstarts and endorsements to 1:1 coaching, loan gear, and insured permits—every detail is covered so riders can focus on progression.

Session flow

How we shape every coaching block.

Structured progressions, feedback on every lap, and deliberate skill stacking ensure riders walk away more prepared than when they arrived.

Regular weekly program

Penrith Mini Bike Club on Mondays & Wednesdays, Dargle Thursday evenings, and full Saturdays at Appin. Mix small-group intensity with private attention—capped at 12 riders for clarity in feedback.

Small group focus

Bike-size and ability-based crews tackle Fast 50 track blocks. Two-hour windows (9–11am or 11:30–1:30pm), max six riders, and track entry with all-day public track access included.

Full-spectrum backing

Kickstarts, endorsements, private 1:1 training, loan helmets and bikes, plus Motorcycling NSW permits that insure riders and spectators. Bookings can be secured with Afterpay.
