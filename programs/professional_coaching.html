Expert Instruction

Regular Weekly After School Program

We run consistent weekly coaching at multiple venues: Penrith Mini Bike Club (Mondays & Wednesdays 4-6:30pm), Dargle (Thursday evenings 4-6:30pm), and Saturdays at Appin all day. Both small group sessions and private coaching available. Maximum of 12 riders per session.

Small Group Coaching at Appin

Specialized sessions for riders of similar bike size and ability on our Fast 50 track. Currently catering to 50cc-125cc bikes with 2-hour sessions (9:00-11:00am or 11:30-1:30pm). Maximum of 6 riders per session. Track entry included, plus all-day access to public tracks.

Comprehensive Support

We provide Motorcycling NSW Kickstarts and Endorsements, offer 1:1 training sessions, and can supply training bikes and helmets when needed. All coaching is done under Motorcycling New South Wales permits, which covers riders and spectators under the insurance policy. Flexible booking with Afterpay available for all coaching programs.
