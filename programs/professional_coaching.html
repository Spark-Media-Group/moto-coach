Professional Coaching

Group coaching sessions across Australia with Level 2 MA accredited coach Leigh Gainfort

Experience professional motocross coaching in a dynamic group environment. Leigh travels to tracks across Australia, bringing decades of racing expertise directly to your local facility. Whether you're a beginner looking to master the basics or an experienced rider aiming to refine your technique, our group coaching sessions provide exceptional value and camaraderie.

Each session accommodates roughly 10-20 riders, creating an energetic learning environment where you can learn from both Leigh's expertise and your fellow riders. Facilities either organise groups through their existing rider communities or advertise Leigh's upcoming visits to attract participants.
