Essential Information

Bike Sourcing

Bikes can be sourced secondhand or new depending on budget. Leigh has extensive contacts and will work with each rider to source a bike to their requirements. Most brands covered. Moto Coach works with ClubMX to support bike sales after the trip.

Exchange Rate Protection

If exchange rate varies more or less by 10%, Moto Coach will pass on the cost as a refund or additional cost. We minimize additional costs by adapting the trip where possible.

Travel Cards & Communication

Carry a Commbank, Up, or Wise travel card for fee-free money access. Purchase eSim prior to travel (we used Holafly) and communicate via WhatsApp to avoid international charges.

Pre-Travel Preparation

One month before departure, we hold a dinner to plan packing, discuss gear, distribute ClubMX/Moto Coach merch, and connect riders and supporters. WhatsApp group set up for ongoing communication.

Travel Requirements

Passport details required for flight booking. Passports need 6 months validity from return date. ESTA visas required. Children under 16 traveling alone need guardian letters.
